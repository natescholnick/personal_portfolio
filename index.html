Personality

I am very confident, which I think is one of the most attractive things about me. That doesen't go without a sparing moment of insufferable hubris too though. A Glimpse of my Academic Past

I composed this list of ten things that I believe or believe in as a "for fun" intro English assignment when I was 16. It does well to exemplify the seriousness with which I regard thought.

  1. People are not created equal, but deserve to be treated as such until known otherwise.
  2. Pride is not a sin; that is silly. However, being proud of inheritance is foolish.
  3. People are rarely overloaded with work, but they’re often overwhelmed.
  4. Humans created God to reconcile ideas greater than themselves.
  5. Each individual only has partial control over their own life, but any control they lack in their life is made up for in the lives of others.
  6. It is ideal to recognize flaws and try to improve them, but there is no shame in having them.
  7. People will rise to high expectations. Contrarily, they’ll succumb to low ones.
  8. To give anything less than your best is to sacrifice the gift.
  9. Ideas and inventions do not need application to be validated. Art exists purely for its own beauty. Following this definition, art has extremely far reaching boundaries.
  10. Myself
